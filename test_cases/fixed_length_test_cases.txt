=== Fixed Length Records Test Cases ===

Test Case 1: Basic Fixed Length Record
Description: Test basic fixed length record reading and writing
Input: 80-character fixed length records
Expected: All records should be read and written correctly

Test Case 2: Short Record Padding
Description: Test padding of short records with spaces
Input: Records shorter than 80 characters
Expected: Records should be padded with spaces to 80 characters

Test Case 3: Long Record Truncation
Description: Test truncation of records longer than 80 characters
Input: Records longer than 80 characters
Expected: Records should be truncated to 80 characters
